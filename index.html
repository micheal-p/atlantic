The Atlantic Seaport City & Cargo Terminal Cross River

The Atlantic Seaport City &
Cargo Terminal Cross River

Welcome to Atlantic Seaport City & Cargo Terminal Cross River, where innovation, sustainability, and excellence converge to set new standards in seaport and urban development. We are leading a transformative project that integrates cutting-edge maritime operations with smart city living, creating a dynamic environment that prioritizes the well-being of our customers, communities, and the environment.

At Atlantic Seaport City & Cargo Terminal, our vision is to establish a premier maritime and urban hub in Africa. We aim to redefine global standards for seaport efficiency, smart city integration, and sustainable development, fostering an ecosystem where business, technology, and community life flourish together.

Our portfolio encompasses a groundbreaking deep-water mega-port terminal and a state-of-the-art smart city located in Cross River State, Nigeria. This ambitious project will not only enhance Nigeria's position in global trade but also provide a vibrant, sustainable living environment for residents and businesses.
