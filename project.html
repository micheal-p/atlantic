The Project

Deep-Water Mega-Port Terminal

The Atlantic Seaport City & Cargo Terminal Cross River is designed to be a multi-faceted development, combining a state-of-the-art seaport with an intelligent, self-sustaining urban environment.

CAPACITY

Our seaport will feature a dredged depth of 60 feet, making it capable of accommodating the largest vessels in the world. This ensures seamless global maritime connectivity and positions us as a pivotal player in international trade.

ADVANCED TECHNOLOGIES

The port will utilize cutting-edge automated terminal technologies, including robotics, artificial intelligence, and IoT solutions, to optimize cargo handling, security, and operational efficiency.

Integrated Smart City

URBAN DESIGN

The smart city will be meticulously planned to integrate residential, commercial, and leisure spaces, providing a holistic living experience. It will feature modern housing, commercial centers, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and green spaces.

SUSTAINABILITY

Emphasis on green building practices, renewable energy sources, and sustainable infrastructure will ensure that the city not only thrives economically but also promotes environmental stewardship.

COMMUNITY AMENITIES

The city will include an entertainment district with sports complexes, museums, parks, and other recreational facilities, creating a vibrant and engaging community for residents and visitors alike.

Economic & Social Impact

JOB CREATION

The project is expected to create over 2 million jobs, encompassing direct employment in port operations and city management, as well as indirect and induced jobs through supplier networks and economic growth.

ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

By establishing a major trading hub, the project will attract international investments, foster local entrepreneurship, and stimulate economic diversification in Cross River State and the wider region.

Infrastructure & Connectivity

TRANSPORT LINKS

Development of a new 50-mile, eight-lane intelligent highway and modern rail lines will ensure efficient connectivity to major cities and industrial hubs, facilitating smooth movement of goods and people.

UTILITIES & SERVICES

State-of-the-art infrastructure for power generation, water distribution, and communication systems will support the needs of both the seaport and the smart city, ensuring reliability and efficiency.
