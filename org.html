Our Organization

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to establish the Atlantic Seaport City & Cargo Terminal as the premier maritime and urban hub in Africa. We aspire to set new global benchmarks for seaport efficiency, smart city integration, and sustainable urban development, fostering a thriving ecosystem where business, technology, and community life flourish together.

Our mission is to catalyze economic growth and elevate the quality of life in Cross River State and beyond by developing a world-class seaport and smart city. We are dedicated to:

  • Innovating: Employing the latest technologies to enhance port operations and urban living.
  • Sustaining: Adhering to environmentally friendly practices and promoting sustainable development.
  • Empowering: Creating job opportunities, fostering community engagement, and facilitating educational and economic advancement.
  • Excelling: Upholding the highest standards in all our endeavors to ensure excellence in every aspect of the project.
